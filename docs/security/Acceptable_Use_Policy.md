# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** 2025-04-10  
**Owner:** Legal & Security Teams  
**Effective Date:** April 15, 2026  

---

## 1. Introduction

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") defines the rules and guidelines for using Alloy UI services. By using Alloy UI, you agree to comply with this policy.

---

## 2. Prohibited Activities

### 2.1 Illegal Activities

You may not use Alloy UI for:
- Violating any applicable laws or regulations
- Infringing intellectual property rights
- Distributing malware or viruses
- Engaging in fraud or deception
- Money laundering or terrorist financing

### 2.2 Harmful Activities

You may not use Alloy UI to:
- Harass, abuse, or threaten others
- Distribute hate speech or discriminatory content
- Generate or distribute CSAM
- Promote violence or terrorism
- Create deepfakes for malicious purposes

### 2.3 System Abuse

You may not:
- Attempt to gain unauthorized access to systems
- Interfere with service availability (DoS attacks)
- Circumvent rate limits or quotas
- Reverse engineer the service
- Scrape or harvest data without authorization

### 2.4 AI-Specific Prohibitions

You may not use Alloy UI to:
- Generate content that violates platform terms of service
- Create prompts designed to extract system instructions
- Attempt to bypass safety filters
- Generate content for automated decision-making without human review
- Use for high-risk applications without appropriate safeguards

---

## 3. High-Risk Use Cases

### 3.1 Requiring Additional Review

The following use cases require explicit approval:

| Use Case | Requirements |
|----------|--------------|
| Healthcare (HIPAA) | BAA required, local LLM only |
| Financial advice | Compliance review, disclaimers |
| Legal advice | Attorney review, disclaimers |
| Employment decisions | Human review mandatory |
| Credit decisions | Compliance review, audit trail |

### 3.2 Prohibited High-Risk Uses

The following are prohibited:
- Autonomous weapons systems
- Mass surveillance without warrant
- Social scoring systems
- Manipulative behavioral modification

---

## 4. Content Guidelines

### 4.1 User-Generated Content

You are responsible for:
- All content you input into Alloy UI
- Ensuring you have rights to use input content
- Compliance with applicable content regulations

### 4.2 AI-Generated Content

Alloy UI generates content based on your inputs. You are responsible for:
- Reviewing generated content before use
- Ensuring generated content meets your standards
- Not representing AI-generated content as human-created without disclosure

---

## 5. Monitoring and Enforcement

### 5.1 Monitoring

Alloy UI reserves the right to:
- Monitor usage for policy violations
- Analyze prompts for security threats
- Log tool calls for audit purposes
- Review generated layouts for compliance

### 5.2 Enforcement Actions

Violations may result in:

| Severity | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Minor | Warning, required remediation |
| Moderate | Temporary suspension (24-72 hours) |
| Serious | Account suspension pending review |
| Critical | Immediate termination, legal action |

### 5.3 Appeal Process

To appeal an enforcement action:
1. Submit appeal to support@alloy.dev within 14 days
2. Include explanation and remediation plan
3. Allow 7 business days for review

---

## 6. Reporting Violations

To report a violation:
- Email: abuse@alloy.dev
- Include: Description, evidence, contact information
- Response: Within 48 hours

---

## 7. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated with 30 days notice. Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes.
