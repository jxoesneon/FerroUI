# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in FerroUI a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints.
- Accepting constructive criticism.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.
- Showing empathy.

**Unacceptable behavior**

- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing others' private information.
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to the project team at **conduct@ferroui.dev**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
