## 7. Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Launch day bugs | Medium | High | Feature freeze 1 week before, war room on standby |
| Negative HN reception | Low | Medium | Engage early with thoughtful comments, have FAQ ready |
| Site traffic overload | Medium | Medium | CDN in place, auto-scaling configured |
| Competitor response | Medium | Low | Focus on differentiation, not competition |
| Message confusion | Medium | High | Clear messaging, consistent across channels |
